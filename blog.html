Kerala University, officially known as the University of Kerala, is a prominent university located in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, India. Established in 1937, it is one of the oldest universities in India and has played a significant role in the development of higher education in the state. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including arts, science, commerce, law, and technology.

It is known for its diverse academic environment, with a strong emphasis on research and innovation. Kerala University is also recognized for its beautiful campus, which is adorned with lush greenery and scenic landscapes. The university is equipped with modern facilities, including well-stocked libraries, laboratories, and sports complexes, providing students with a conducive environment for learning and personal development.

The university has several affiliated colleges spread across the state, contributing to its mission of making higher education accessible to a broader audience. Additionally, it has established collaborations with various national and international institutions, promoting academic exchange and research initiatives.
